Full description:
This application adds exo Oka Express bus information to MonTransit.

This app contains the buses schedule (available offline).

exo Oka Express serves Oka, Saint-Eustache, Saint-Joseph-du-Lac and Saint-Placide.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display bus information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM).
https://rtm.quebec/en/about/open-data

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-deux-montagnes-mrcdm-bus-android

This app is not related with the Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM), the Autorité Régionale de Transport Métropolitain (ARTM) and exo Oka Express.

exo Oka Express was formely know as RTM Oka Express sector and MRC Deux-Montagnes.
exo was formely know as Réseau de Transport Métropolitain (RTM) and Agence Métropolitaine de Transport (AMT).
